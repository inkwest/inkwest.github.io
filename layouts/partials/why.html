Why Inkwest?

Zero setup for developers

No VPN, no kubeconfig, no kubectl install, no MCP server. Developers open a browser and start debugging — the infrastructure team handles connectivity once.

Tighter permissions

Stop handing out broad cluster access "just in case". Inkwest acts as a controlled read-only proxy — access is scoped through OIDC and role mappings, not kubeconfigs.

Single-user or team

Run locally in dev mode with auto-login for personal use, or deploy with OIDC for your entire team. Same tool, same experience.
